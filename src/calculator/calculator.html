Calculator Help

How to Use the Calculator

  1. Search and select articles from the left panel
  2. Review selected articles in the center panel
  3. Click "Calculate" to compute total punishments
  4. View detailed breakdown in the results panel
  5. Save, export, or copy your calculations

Features

  • Smart Parsing: Automatically extracts fine and jail time from text
  • Range Calculations: Shows minimum and maximum penalties
  • Multiple Formats: Supports various punishment formats
  • Export Options: PDF, text, and clipboard copying
  • History: Save and recall previous calculations

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+K: Open calculator
  • Ctrl+N: New calculation
  • Ctrl+Shift+C: Copy results
  • Escape: Close calculator
